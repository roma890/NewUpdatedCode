Comparing Fuel Prices for FT2

Explore the average annual fuel costs for different fuel types across various vehicles. The horizontal axis represents Fuel Type 2, including Propane, E85, Natural Gas, and Electricity. The vertical axis represents the Average Annual Fuel Cost (in dollars), ranging from 0 to 3500+. Use this graph to compare the cost efficiency of different fuel types for FT2.

From the graph, we can see that Propane by far has the highest average annual fuel cost at over 3500. Electric has the lowest annual fuel cost at around 150. Electric seems like the best option when it comes to having a low annual fuel cost for Fuel Type 2.
